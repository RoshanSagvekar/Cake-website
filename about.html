Jack Martin

Founder

When I opened The Fresh Ovenhot Cake Shoppe in 2010, I began with the question, “What makes people smile?” I found that what brings a smile to any face is the perfectly baked treat, and while my business has grown and changed, this focus has stayed the same. My team and I strive to serve in a way that meets your goal. We believe that each sweet treat is an opportunity to bring joy to another person’s world.

If You Can Dream It, We Can Bake It.

At the core of my bakery is the belief that a hand-made treat can change any moment into a special occasion, and any special occasion into a memory. That’s why The Ovenhot Cake Shoppe offers our customers a uniquely personal buying experience. We work with you one-on-one to determine what the cake or dessert of your dreams is, and what we must do to fulfill that dream. Whether your heart’s desire is a simple treat, a Volcano cake, or a 8-tier wedding cake, our team of master decorators will always be ready and able to create the perfect cake or dessert for your special occasion.
